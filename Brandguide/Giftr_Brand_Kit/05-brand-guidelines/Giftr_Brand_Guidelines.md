# Giftr Brand Guidelines

## Brand essence

Giftr makes gifting effortless by giving creators and communities an autonomous agent that can search, buy, pay for, poll delivery, and send redemption information without manual work.

## Tagline

**AI agents that buy and deliver gifts automatically.**

## Elevator pitch

Giftr is an autonomous AI gifting agent built for community raffles, rewards, and thoughtful one-off gifts. It receives an intent, searches Bitrefill-compatible products, selects the best gift, creates and pays the invoice, polls delivery, validates redemption, and emails the recipient automatically.

## Visual personality

- **Trustworthy** — clean, precise UI and clear state visibility.
- **Fast** — orange motion lines and crisp interaction patterns imply speed.
- **Joyful** — sparkle, gift cues, and light celebration details add warmth.
- **Clear** — simple language and readable product screens.
- **Agentic** — the system acts for the user, end-to-end.

## Logo

The Giftr mark combines a fast-moving digital gift with a small sparkle. The icon should feel like gifting, delivery motion, and agentic commerce, without becoming childish gift-box clipart.

### Logo files

- `logo-primary.svg` — main horizontal lockup.
- `logo-stacked.svg` — vertical/narrow layouts.
- `logo-icon.svg` — favicon, app icon, compact UI.
- `logo-wordmark.svg` — text-only use.
- `logo-reversed.svg` — dark backgrounds.
- `logo-monochrome-black.svg` and `logo-monochrome-white.svg` — restricted color use.

## Typography

Recommended pairing:

- Display / headings: **Sora** or a similar rounded geometric sans.
- UI / body: **Inter** or a similar highly legible interface sans.

Fallback stack:

```css
font-family: Inter, Sora, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif;
```

## UI tone

Use bright surfaces, soft shadows, modest rounded corners, and real product UI. Giftr should look like a polished SaaS product rather than a crypto trading dashboard.

## Status language

- Success: Delivered, Payment confirmed, Redemption delivered.
- Pending: Polling delivery, Waiting for code, In progress.
- Error: Pending payment, Redemption failed, Needs attention.
- Neutral: Scheduled, Not started, Inactive.

## Recommended CTAs

- Start a raffle
- Run Raffle
- Send gift
- Watch demo
- View delivery
- Export results

## Do

- Use the official logo files.
- Keep the sparkle with the icon.
- Use teal for trust/actions and orange for primary CTAs/energy.
- Show agent steps transparently.
- Keep copy short and product-led.

## Don’t

- Do not copy or imitate Bitrefill branding.
- Do not use generic robot mascots.
- Do not make the UI feel like a crypto trading terminal.
- Do not overuse confetti or childish gift illustrations.
- Do not stretch, recolor, or add effects to the logo.
